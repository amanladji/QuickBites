❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got honest answers.

How quickly will my food arrive?

Our average delivery time is 28 minutes. Most orders arrive within 20–35 minutes depending on your location, the restaurant's prep time, and current traffic conditions. You can track your delivery live on the map once the order is picked up.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major payment methods: UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and Cash on Delivery. All digital payments are secured with bank-grade PCI-DSS encryption.

Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?

You can cancel or modify your order within 2 minutes of placing it, before the restaurant accepts it. Once accepted, cancellations may be subject to a small fee. Contact our support for assistance.

What is the minimum order amount?

Minimum order amounts vary by restaurant, typically between ₹100 – ₹300. The minimum order for each restaurant is clearly displayed on their menu page before you add items to the cart.

How do QuickCoins work?

Every time you order on QuickBite, you earn 10 QuickCoins per order. 10 QuickCoins = ₹10 off your next order. Reach Gold status (50+ orders) to unlock free delivery on all future orders and exclusive member-only deals.

What if my order arrives late or is wrong?

We guarantee your satisfaction. If your order arrives more than 15 minutes late or if there's any issue with your food, contact support within 24 hours for an instant refund or replacement — no questions asked.

Do you offer contactless delivery?

Yes! Select "Contactless Delivery" at checkout. Your delivery partner will leave the order at your door, take a photo as proof of delivery, and notify you via the app. No contact needed.
